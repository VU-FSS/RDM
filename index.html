This is a draft version of the VU FSS Getting Started guide. The goal is to have a quick overview of all the things the research office can do for researchers, presented in a way that it is easy to navigate, even for people not necessarily familiar with the way we work. For now, I have included the names of RSO people after each link, to indicate who is responsible for the content.

Some questions:

  • Are the phases of the research cycle correct, or should we add more?
  • Are there topics that we provide support for that we should add?
  • Are the names of the pages easy to understand? How can we improve?
